PARIS

Paris is the capital and largest city of France, situated on the Seine River in the northern part of the country. Known as the "City of Light," it is a global center for finance, culture, fashion, and gastronomy, boasting iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre

TOKYO

Tokyo,[a] officially the Tokyo Metropolis,[b] is the capital and most populous city in Japan. With a population of over 14 million in the city proper in 2023, it is one of the most populous urban areas in the world. The Greater Tokyo Area, which includes Tokyo and parts of six neighboring prefectures, is the most populous metropolitan area in the world, with 41 million residents as of 2024.

new york

New York, often called New York City (NYC),[b] is the most populous city in the United States. It is located at the southern tip of New York State on one of the world's largest natural harbors. The city comprises five boroughs, each coextensive with its respective county.
